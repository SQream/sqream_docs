July 2022
==================
The following enhancements were introduced in :ref:`July 2022 (2022.1) <2022.1>`:

.. list-table::
   :widths: 20 20 60
   :header-rows: 1  
   
   * - Topic
     - Feature
     - Description
   * - **Data Ingestion**
     - `Inserting data from Avro <https://docs.sqream.com/en/v2022.1/data_ingestion/avro.html#avro>`_
     - SQream now supports ingesting data from Avro files.
   * - **Feature Guides**
     - `Data encryption <https://docs.sqream.com/en/v2022.1/feature_guides/data_encryption.html#data-encryption>`_
     - SQream has integrated data encryption mechanisms in accordance with General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards.
   * - **Reference Guides**
     - `UPDATE <https://docs.sqream.com/en/v2022.1/reference/sql/sql_statements/dml_commands/update.html#update>`_
     - The DML ``UPDATE`` statement has been introduced to support modifying the value of certain columns in existing rows.
